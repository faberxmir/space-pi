04
Principles
how we dress the ship
001 · phosphor, not photoshop

Everything is transmitted, not rendered. Scanlines, a soft bloom on light text, and a near-imperceptible flicker should always be present. No drop shadows. No glassmorphism. No gradients except the phosphor glow itself.

002 · monospace as grid

Type is the grid. Align by columns of 8. Prefer ASCII box-drawing and dashed dividers to boxes and borders. Corners are square. Radii are for enemies.

003 · data is drama

Latencies, coordinates, packet loss and star dates are the ornament. If a slot looks empty, add a readout, not a decoration. Every number should feel real — use live values where you can.
